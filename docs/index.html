CPC Advisory The official NOAA/CPC ENSO Diagnostic Discussion, issued monthly. An El Niño or La Niña Advisory is declared when the Oceanic Niño Index (ONI) meets or exceeds ±0.5 °C for five consecutive overlapping 3-month seasons.

Synopsis: El Niño conditions are present and expected to strengthen into the Northern Hemisphere winter 2026-27.

ONI vs RONI — Last 3 Years (Monthly) ONI (Oceanic Niño Index) is the standard 3-month running mean of the Niño-3.4 anomaly — the primary metric NOAA uses to classify El Niño and La Niña events. RONI (Relative ONI) applies a linear detrending to remove the long-term warming signal.
